Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I turn up the volume?
  • How do I change the chime sound?
  • How do I find answers not listed here?

How do I turn up the volume?

The chime volume is set by the ringtone volume. Increase in decrease your ringer volume to change the chime volume.

How do I change the chime sound?

As of version 2.0 the chime cannot be changed, but it is planned for a future release.

How do I find answers not listed here?

Touch on the "More" tab at the bottom of your screen, then touch "Support & Feedback" to ask your question.
