How do I turn up the volume?

When the app is open, the volume of the chime is tied to the music volume even is no music is playing. If not music if playing, the volume buttons may not work, but you can swipe up from the bottom of the screen to access the volume from the command center.

How do I find answers not listed here?

Touch on the "More" tab at the bottom of your screen, then touch "Support & Feedback" to ask your question.
